3D Study Pink Donut Box

This 3D study is an homage to the legendary story behind the Los Angeles pink donut box. Navigating a new digital medium, the project represents a deliberate creative challenge: translating a physically nostalgic, culturally rich piece of SoCal history into the precise world of 3D design.

About the Artist

Hi, my name is Alma Contreras. I am a multidisciplinary artist living in Los Angeles. My work intertwines common symbols and imagery from daily life rituals with the goal of exploring the never-static vitality of life.
